Who we are ?
Our Stand

We believe in God the Father, God the Son and God the Holy Spirit. We stand in the inseparable of the Trinity and the supremacy of the power therein over all creations. That thereafter, we shall return unto God to give stewardship of our existence and be rewarded accordingly.

Matt. 3:16-17, II Cor. 5:10

Learn more

Our Mission

To follow and fulfil the directive of our Lord Jesus Christ, to preach the gospel to all nations.

Matt. 28:19-20

Our Vision

To ensure that everyone we meet through the gospel is a safe soul and a candidate of heaven, such soul should also experience a fulfilled live on earth and become a source of blessing to others.

Matt. 25:31-46

Evangelism

Our passion for evangelism over the years has taken us far and wide, in meeting the yearnings of lost souls. Especially in the rural areas of the society where many have been neglected. We have won souls, but will not relent in doing more to ensure that those Souls remain in Christ.

John 4:39-42
