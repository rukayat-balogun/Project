Praise Services

We hold Praise Services as many times as possible within the year in appreciation of the goodness of God over the Ministry and for the lives of every member, believing that things will continue to turn out good for us. Even when prayers have not yet been fully answered, we appreciate God for He hears us and He is there for us. So, we are happy people who by all standard understand appreciation unto the Lord, who was, who is and who will be forever. Praise Services are period to merry, wine and dine, extending hand of fellowship to others with love, hoping for the best in days ahead.

I Tess. 5:18

Evangelism

Our passion for evangelism over the years has taken us far and wide in meeting the yearnings of lost souls especially in the rural areas of the society where many have been neglected. We have won souls, but will not relent in doing more to ensure that those souls remain in Christ.

John 4:39-42

Agape Visitation

Converted souls are not left alone. We continually visit them on individual basis and as a group, depending on circumstances, calls are put across to them from time to time, words of encouragement sent to those that are far away and we have never stopped praying for them.

Luke 10:38-42

Prayer Time

We meet every Mondays from 8am - 10am for an Open-Air Inter-Denominational Prayer Session. The day and time was fixed for those who knows and are ready to pray, having it in mind that aggressive prayers are the only solutions to their numerous problems. It is for serious minded people seeking lasting solution to their challenges of life and, testimonies have been the order of the day among attendees. We also observe Special Prayer Vigil monthly, from 10pm till 5am of the day of the month which represents the month numerical number (e.g January 1, February 2...). We also have Wednesday Prayer Time from 10am - 12noon and thereafter, Counselling Session.

I Tess. 5:16-17

Open Air Crusade

The Ministry holds Open Air Crusade twice a year. This bi-annual programme is held to bring together every believer from different religion backgrounds to edifice God’s name among the people, and to evangelise to a larger crowd of the community where the program is held. Various Anointed Servants of God hold various sessions during the Crusade that lasts for not less than 3 days.

Mark 1:14-15

Benevolence

If there is any other thing alongside spreading the Gospel that the Ministry is committed to, it is the act of giving to the less privileged ones in the society. The Ministry identifies among the people those who are genuinely in need of financial assistance and their needs are met. Hospital bills are settled, school fees are paid, empowerment programme carried out, food stuff distributed to the widows and others, gifts to motherless babies homes.

Act 2:44-47

Believers' Class

It is mandatory for all converted souls to attend Believer's Class, to root them in the Word. Teachings are only established on the facts from the Bible, and various bothering questions are answered in reference to the Bible. Anything aside the Bible is not welcome and members are taught to make the Bible their reference.

Luke 4:15-34
