Praise Services

We hold Praise Services as many times as possible within the year in appreciation of the goodness of God over the Ministry and the lives of every member, believing that things will continue to turn out well. Even when prayers are yet unanswered, we praise God for hearing us. We are a joyous community of believers who appreciate the Lord, who was, who is, and who will be forever.

I Thess. 5:18

Evangelism

Our passion for evangelism has led us to reach out to lost souls, especially in rural areas. We are committed to keeping souls in Christ.

John 4:39-42

Agape Visitation

We continually support converted souls with visitations, encouragement, and prayers to keep them rooted in faith.

Luke 10:38-42

Prayer Time

We meet every Monday from 8am to 10am for an Open-Air Inter-Denominational Prayer Session. Monthly, we hold a Special Prayer Vigil from 10pm to 5am. Every Wednesday, we gather from 10am to 12noon for prayer followed by a Counseling Session.

I Thess. 5:16-17

Open Air Crusade

The Ministry holds Open Air Crusades twice a year, gathering believers from various backgrounds to glorify God and spread His message.

Mark 1:14-15

Benevolence

The Ministry actively supports the less privileged with financial aid, medical assistance, school fees, and food distributions.

Acts 2:44-47

Believers' Class

New converts are required to attend Believers' Class to deepen their knowledge of the Word, with a focus solely on the Bible.

Luke 4:15-34
